Mission Statement

VetNestUSA’s mission is to connect veterans with safe housing, supportive services, and resources they can trust. Our platform is free for veterans, with sponsors and providers supporting our mission through affordable listing plans.

About the Founder

Kevin Kitchens, a 100% combat-disabled veteran and former Airborne Combat Engineer, founded VetNestUSA to give back to the veteran community. Drawing from personal experience, Kevin ensures the platform maintains its integrity, accessibility, and mission-first focus.

Listing Standards

  • No false or misleading advertisements
  • All listings must benefit veterans directly
  • Respectful and professional conduct is required
  • VetNestUSA reserves the right to remove any listing that violates these standards
